PAIN

I sat on the bridge, my feet dangling on the edge. I looked at the lake beneath me. The bridge's reflection was beautiful. I ran my hand down the vines of the sakuras. The sakuras are dead, dead petals fell at my touch, describing my state at the moment. I was dead. Feeling less. Blind and mute. Not physically of course. Emotionally. Every word that has been said to me were words of discouragement and hatred. Today was the anniversary of my separation with Hani, Lina and Ecli. I concluded on this day, I can't carry on by myself. They fill me. They are friends. Friends I never had. I looked at the neat pile of diaries in front of me. Each of them had at least 5 things about how much I cared about them. And why they threw me out like rubbish is still a mystery. But I'm a fool and I want them back into my life. Everyone eventually left my life. Hani, Lina,Cease,Ecli, Aeel and my family. Now I'm all alone, in the dark. I smiled as my tears stung my eyes. I know where this was going to head, as tears poured out like an endless stream of water. I looked at the lake again, allowing my tears to mix with the water. At least a part of me is in there. I stood up on the rails of the bridge. The vines prickled my feet. I looked back, through the window and into the restaurant, where there were 4 girls laughing. Hani turned around as she saw a familiar smile as the figure lifted its leg and fell from the railing. The fall was followed with a splash. Hani ran out and looked down, watching the girl she and the others hurt so much, slowly sinking into the lake. She trembled and shook. Tears started busting out. Then she screamed a blood curling scream
