ABOUT ME...OR HER

Hi! My name is Fatima Farah. I'm a online writer by the name of Kim Farah, my pen name. This is about Me, Myself and I. I am a Muslim Teenager.I live in Melbourne with my Mother and Sister. I study at DUCV, short for Darul Ulum College of Victoria. It's got a pretty reputation, I guess. The place I live in is currently Melbourne, Victoria. I'm from Bangladesh though. I like it in Australia, all my friends and stuff, but I enjoy it more in Bangladesh with the rest of my family. People say I'm weird but the only thing you got to know before you read through this website about me is that I use my own language. I use English,yes, but I use my words in different contexts. This website is about me, my life,my future careers, my dreams and basically all about me. Why? I'm me, that's why. I am a official writer at Wattpad and a author at Write4Fun. This website contains no personal info, I'm afraid so (i don't want to be stalked down). Just click those options on top to see me, myself and I and my life.
