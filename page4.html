NOT WHO I AM

A girl... Taerah Farah. A boy...Kim Taehyung. Taerah... a Muslim. Taehyung... a Christian.
Taerah is an ARMY and is willing to give up anything for Kim Taehyung.....her bias and her long-term crush. It's not really every day you love a celebrity for 4 years. When Taerah meets Taehyung in a fan meet, she was struck with happiness. A happiness that won't come when you just see your celebrity. The happiness you get when you meet the one you love. Taehyung, who is slightly different from his personality on TV, avoids and stays away from Taerah because she is a Muslim. That's when Taerah starts to change. She stops wearing an Abaya and Scarf and starts wearing T-shirts and Tops?! Taehyung notices how she could give up her own religion for her love for him. But you know someone always has to come in the way... Suga, who thinks Taerah is beautiful in her own way falls for her. Even when she changes he can't get him out of his head. Jennie, who is a girl who liked Taehyung from the first she laid her eyes on him. She will do anything to get Fatima out of her way. Taerah is not Taerah But, Kim Farah. Now all attention goes to her. And Jennie will do anything to make sure Kim Farah never gets in her way. She and her group of 4 friends, whose words are like poison can do anything. But Farah will cope and in many ways....
