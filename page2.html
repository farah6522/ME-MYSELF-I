THUS BEGINS MY STORY OF THE MANY DREAMS I'VE HAD...

2013-2014

When I was small, I had a great fascination of stars, I used to think "Waaahhh! These balls of gas in the sky are so awesome!! I'd love to know and discover new stuff about them!!" I used to think there are hidden things about them yet to be discovered. At times when I'd feel sad, I'd look up at the stars to calm myself down. I don't know...stars just seemed so beautiful, so white, so powerful, full of secrets. I'd even pretend talking to the stars sometimes!! I always was fascinated by outer space and have always wondered what it contained.




2015-2016

Back in 2015 I wanted to be an artist. I'd always dream of penning my imaginations and turning them real through my drawings and my paintbrushes. Each piece of art would take me down to a different journey in my mind, to a different world, a different land. I loved the fact that I could bring my dreams to live in paper. My heart would beat at the thought of a new piece, a new art, a new life. To be an artist...was my second dream.

2017-2018

Writing. My true passion that I discovered till now. I just knew. It was Singing and Writing. I couldn't believe I didn't notice it. I had a passion, extreme. I love to sing. I love to write. And that's why I love to lyric-write as well. I thing writing is a way to express yourself so people can understand. I loved poetry because it was the biggest form of expressing and revealing yourself. Singing, is my priority over writing. Music is my passion, my first priority. The tune stops me from crying. Sometimes I just wonder if I am music and Music is Me. We seem so compatible. It's a way that you can express yourself just by one or two sounds from your mouth! I've had inspiration and my love for music came from BTS. I am forever thankful to them that they brought me and put me where I belong and showed me who I am. Music is me and I am Music.
