KIM FARAH?

My name is Fatima Farah. Then, who is Kim Farah? Fatima is a person, who needs defending. Who needs some help. She's helpless. She's sad. Kim Farah is her friend, her defender, helpful, happy. She's a friend. But she is Fatima Farah. Confusing right? Fatima is Kim Farah but different personality. Fatima loves to draw, and be quite, she can't stand a loud place. On the other hand, Kim Farah is probably the most loudest girl a person can bare. She loves sports, singing and writing. A naughty version of Fatima she is. Fatima is always oppressed and lonely. Kim Farah is free and strong. She's the wind that makes the red leaves fall at autumn. Fatima is the red leaves. Kim Farah's the Sun that burns the plants. Fatima is the plant. This is how the cycle works. She can be whichever she wants anytime. But at night she's both, watching the stars. A friendship. Where did the name Kim Farah come from? Thank BTS and Lee Euna. Lee Euna gaveme the name Kim Farah Young. And thank BTS for creating Kim Farah Young. :)
